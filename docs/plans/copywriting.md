## 6. Contrast Feature: Spambot AI vs. Value-First Copy

> [!NOTE]
> **Layout Concept:** A side-by-side comparison slider showing what standard AI writes vs. what GrowthOS writes for a Reddit community.

* **Section Headline:**
  ## How to promote without getting banned.

````carousel
### Standard ChatGPT Prompt (Fluffy & High Risk)
```
Hey React Native devs! 🚀 Check out this AMAZING starter kit that will make your life 10x easier!!! It has everything you need. Hundreds of developers are already loving it. Trust me, it's a game-changer! Go to StarterKit.io now! 🔥
```
* **Status:** 🔴 Banned instantly by Automoderator.
* **Reason:** Sounds like a spambot, makes fake claims ("hundreds of developers"), offers zero community value, uses excessive emojis.
<!-- slide -->
### GrowthOS Generated Post (Honest & Value-First)
```
Hey everyone, I'm the builder of [StarterKit]. I spent the last month setting up standard auth, Supabase integration, and Tailwind styling for my React Native projects. 

Instead of copying and pasting this setup for the fifth time, I bundled it into a starter template. I want to make sure the onboarding is smooth, so I'm looking for 10 builders to test it out for free and tell me where it breaks. 

I've documented the architecture details below if you want to inspect it first.
```
* **Status:** 🟢 Upvoted & Approved.
* **Reason:** Discloses builder status, explains the context/problem, offers real value, and invites honest feedback without fake claims.
````

## 9. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

* **Section Headline:**
  ## Frequently Asked Questions

* **Q: Is GrowthOS just a wrapper around ChatGPT?**
  * **A:** No. Generic AI models don't know what communities are active right now, nor can they organize their thoughts. GrowthOS runs real-time search queries to find live, human-moderated communities and formats the results into a persistent, local database structure (`Goal -> Channels -> Plans -> Todos`) designed to manage your launch workflow step-by-step.

* **Q: Will using GrowthOS copy get me banned?**
  * **A:** We design our copywriting prompts to do the opposite. GrowthOS has built-in "Adversarial Judges" that evaluate copy drafts against community guidelines. The system flags hype, forces developer disclosure, and ensures you write value-first posts that spark conversation rather than get flagged as spam.

* **Q: Do I need a credit card to sign up?**
  * **A:** No. You can create your first campaign dashboard, search for target communities, and get your first 15 structured todos completely free. We only require a subscription if you need unlimited campaigns, advanced copywriting evals, and full tool integration.

* **Q: Can I share my campaign roadmap with others?**
  * **A:** Yes! GrowthOS lets you generate a public, read-only link to your campaign dashboard so you can share your launch progress and todos with your Twitter followers or community buddies.
