## 1. Subscription Plans (Pro, Agency, Hibernate)

- **Monthly plans:** Cancel anytime; you keep access until the end of the paid billing period.
  No partial-month refunds for unused time.
- **Annual plans:** Refundable within **14 days** of purchase or renewal if you have generated
  fewer than 1 full campaign (10 AI credits) during that period. After 14 days, or above that
  usage, annual plans are non-refundable but you retain access through the paid term.
- **Downgrades:** Take effect at the next billing cycle; no partial refund for the difference.

## 2. Free Plan

No payment, no refund applicable.

## 3. AI Credit Top-Ups

Pay-as-you-go credit purchases ($10/100 credits, $25/300 credits) are **non-refundable once any
credit from that top-up has been consumed** (a credit is consumed the moment an agent run — goal
analysis, channel research, copywriting draft, web search update — completes). Unused, fully
untouched top-ups may be refunded within 7 days of purchase.

## 4. Billing Errors

If you're charged in error (duplicate charge, wrong plan applied, charge after cancellation), we
will refund the erroneous charge in full once verified. Report billing errors within 60 days of
the charge.

## 5. Failed or Unsatisfactory AI Output

AI-generated plans, copy, and research can be wrong or low-quality. This is a product-quality
issue, not a billing issue — it does not itself trigger a refund. Use the "regenerate" flow to
retry the affected plan/channel at no charge, or contact support if a run failed to execute
correctly (e.g., errored before producing output) — in that case the consumed credits will be
restored.

## 6. How to Request a Refund

Email [billing/support email] with your account email and the reason for the request. We aim to
resolve refund requests within 5 business days.

## 7. Chargebacks

If you dispute a charge with your bank/card issuer instead of contacting us first, we reserve the
right to suspend your account pending resolution.

## 8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as billing infrastructure and plans evolve. Material changes will be
posted with a new effective date and won't apply retroactively to charges already made.
