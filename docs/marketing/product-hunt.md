## Description

GrowthOS turns a raw goal ("get my first 50 beta testers") into a structured growth campaign — the specific communities your audience is actually in, prioritized todos, and draft copy for each one.

What makes it different from asking ChatGPT for a marketing plan:

- **Live web search, not stale lists** — finds the actual subreddits, Discord servers, and directories active right now
- **Structured, not a wall of text** — Goal → Channels → Plans → Todos, saved to a dashboard you check off, not a chat log you lose
- **Built-in guardrails** — every draft is checked against rules that block hype, fabricated stats, and undisclosed self-promotion, so it doesn't get you banned from the one community that fits
- **You stay in control** — GrowthOS drafts and recommends, you post it yourself; nothing auto-publishes

Free during early access, no card.
