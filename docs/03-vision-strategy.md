## Why this design works

Each layer has a single responsibility:

- **Campaign** → The overall initiative.
- **Goal** → The measurable outcome you're trying to achieve.
- **Channel** → The audience or distribution opportunity that was selected.
- **Plan** → The strategy for succeeding in that specific channel.
- **Todo** → The actionable work items needed to execute the plan.

This structure is also future-proof. You can later add AI agents that own individual plans, regenerate only a failed plan, or automatically create new plans when the AI discovers additional channels—all without changing the overall campaign model.
